# Ivory Color

Ivory is a soft, off-white shade with a hint of warmth, reminiscent of the material it is named after. This color exudes elegance and sophistication, making it a popular choice in both fashion and interior design.

## Psychology

Ivory is known to:
- Evoke feelings of purity and tranquility
- Create a sense of warmth and comfort
- Impart a timeless and classic aesthetic

## Usage

This versatile color works well in:
- Wedding and event themes for a romantic atmosphere
- Interior design as a neutral backdrop
- Fashion for an elegant and refined look
- Branding for luxury and high-end products

## Combinations

Ivory pairs beautifully with:
- Soft Taupe (#D1C6B5)
- Dusty Rose (#D4A5A5)
- Sage Green (#B2C8B2)
- Deep Charcoal (#333333)
