# Ocher Color

Ocher is a warm, earthy hue that ranges from yellow to brown, often resembling the color of natural clay. This color is associated with the richness of the earth and evokes a sense of organic warmth and stability.

## Psychology

Ocher Color is known to:
- Promote feelings of warmth and comfort
- Encourage a sense of grounding and stability
- Inspire creativity and connection to nature

## Usage

This versatile color works well in:
- Interior design for creating cozy and inviting spaces
- Branding for companies focused on sustainability and natural products
- Fashion, particularly in autumn collections
- Art and design, often used to create earthy palettes

## Combinations

Ocher Color pairs beautifully with:
- Deep Green (#4B8B3B)
- Soft Blue (#A4C8E1)
- Terracotta (#D76D3D)
- Cream (#F5F5DC)
