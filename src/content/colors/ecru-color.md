# Ecru Color

Ecru is a warm, neutral shade that falls between beige and light brown. It resembles the natural color of unbleached linen and evokes a sense of simplicity, warmth, and organic beauty.

## Psychology

Ecru is known to:
- Promote feelings of calmness and serenity
- Evoke a sense of stability and reliability
- Create a cozy and inviting atmosphere

## Usage

This versatile color works well in:
- Interior design for a soft, warm backdrop
- Fashion and textile applications as a neutral base
- Branding for companies that focus on sustainability and natural products
- Wedding palettes for a romantic and elegant touch

## Combinations

Ecru pairs beautifully with:
- Olive Green (#4F6D7A)
- Dusty Rose (#D3B6B0)
- Slate Gray (#708090)
- Soft White (#F8F8F8)
