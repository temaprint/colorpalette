# Saffron Color

Saffron color is a rich and vibrant shade of yellow-orange, reminiscent of the precious spice derived from the flower of Crocus sativus. This color radiates warmth and energy, often associated with luxury and exoticism.

## Psychology

Saffron Color is known to:
- Evoke feelings of warmth and optimism
- Stimulate creativity and enthusiasm
- Promote a sense of adventure and exploration

## Usage

This striking color is ideal for use in:
- Culinary branding and food packaging
- Fashion and accessories, especially in vibrant collections
- Interior design, particularly in creating focal points
- Marketing materials aiming to capture attention and convey energy

## Combinations

Saffron Color pairs beautifully with:
- Deep Blue (#003B5C)
- Olive Green (#3D9970)
- Charcoal Gray (#333333)
- Cream (#FFFDD0)
